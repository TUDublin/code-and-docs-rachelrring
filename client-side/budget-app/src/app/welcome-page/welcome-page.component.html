Welcome!

This site is made for you to explore your financial situation and to view a breakdown of your income and expenses.
This site should be used as an eye opening tool to see where your moeny is going!

Overview

On our Overview page you can see the Irish national averages for both income and expenses. If you are logged in and have created your budget, you will see your very own personalised recommendations!

Budget Planner

Use our interactive tool to see how much money you have coming in and where it's all going.
If you are logged in, you will be able to save your income and expenses data to comeback and update it at a later date.

Budget Breakdown

Explore detailed insights into your spending habits, understand where your money goes, and identify ways to cut costs and save.
You will be shown how much each expense contribute to your overall expenditure.
